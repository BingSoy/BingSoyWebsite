Humans don't seem to like being bullied and may do something drastic if unaddressed. Build mental insulation against bad actors via friends, family, mental discipline, physical strength training, relevant authorities if applicable.

But it's never that simple. Bullying is a much harder issue to attack if you are someone in the middle of it/younger/don't have the tools or knowledge to combat it.

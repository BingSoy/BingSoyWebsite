Glossary of game/video title terms

Some terms may confuse the unacquainted.

Terms:

  • SA = Silent Assassin
  • SO = Suit Only - any of Agent 47's suits you start with in a mission (starting with disguises or changing disguises invalidates a SO run, unsure but an exception may be the tuxedo for auction starting location at paris)
  • SASO = Silent Assassin, Suit Only
  • Casual, Professional and Master = game difficulty levels, master being hardest
  • IOI = IO Interactive - Copenhagen game company who made the Hitman/HITMAN games.

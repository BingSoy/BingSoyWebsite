Reliance on online servers

IOI the game unnecessarily reliant on internet connection, effectively cutting out various features and progression for the game for "offline mode"/anti-cheat. All for the reason of "cheaters".
This isn't good for:

  • People who want to play offline/don't want to waste unnecessary bandwidth (data caps)
  • People with bad/no internet connection
  • Long term videogame preservation, especially when game's popularity dies/IOI HITMAN servers die (maybe the peacock project will continue do what IOI can't)

Some things cut from offline mode (some may be missing from list):

  • Escalations
  • Scoring screen
  • Campaign mode (HITMAN 3)
  • Progression and unlocks

I don't agree with the whole premise/principle of cutting stuff out for the sake of cheated leaderboards/player profiles (if that's truly the reason), especially since leaderboards are disabled anyway in offline mode and despite that, you're not even allowed to see your own score due to being offline in this singleplayer game.
Servers don't last forever.

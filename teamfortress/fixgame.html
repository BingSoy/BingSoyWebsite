Fix Team Fortress 2

Below are ideas & suggestions of varying priority regarding TF2 game balance with an emphasis on Valve's casual servers. Bing Soy may not necessarily represent the whole TF2 community's will, ideas here are an amalgamation of years of personal TF2 gaming experience and the watching of some TF2 creators to gain more perspective.

General game balance

These are merely suggestions to help spark dialogue as to how the game can be updated to make it more fair & fresh, therefore more fun and rewarding. Many of these suggestions could very well be bad for the game's health, do not blindly think that these are bulletproof ideas.
This list could be neater and more well thought out, but Bing Soy wants it out there.

  • Remove wrangler damage resistance:  The manual control is just fine. The damage resistance turns annoying turtling into something stupidly tanky for a simple weapon switch. It counters the counters to sentries (snipers, LnL, Direct Hit).
  • Make sniper rifles "bullets" into projectiles, like a faster huntsman projectile. Currently, all sniper rifles currently work like lasers (hitscan). Make supportive than just broken laser gameplay denier class? Maybe have some status effects according to which bodypart was hit.? Arm = switchspeed penalty, legs = slow, chest = bleed. Nerf sniper damage, why instant headshot laser?
  • Pyro vs sentry counterplay: Currently, Pyro has atrocious counterplay to sentries. Buff flaregun damage toward buildings? Have flamethrower flames cause sentry inaccuracy/targeting delay when flames block sentry sightline? (pyro is next to sentry and outpositioned/vulnerable/conspicious when doing this) Honorable mention: Dragon's Fury (should learn and use more to observe effectiveness).
